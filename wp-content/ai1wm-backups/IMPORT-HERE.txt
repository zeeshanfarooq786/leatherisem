DO NOT use "Import" upload in WordPress admin for large files.

Instead:
1. Copy your full .wpress file HERE (should be ~366 MB, not 11 MB)
2. Restart Apache in XAMPP
3. Run from command line:
   cd c:\xampp\htdocs\leatherisem
   c:\xampp\php\php.exe ai1wm-restore.php

Or open in browser (logged in as admin):
   http://localhost/leatherisem/ai1wm-restore.php
